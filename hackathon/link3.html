Emergency helpline

  • AASRA: AASRA is a Mumbai-based organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services. They offer a 24/7 helpline for individuals in distress. Helpline: 91-9820466726.
  • SNEHI: Snehi is a Delhi-based organization offering emotional support and counseling services to individuals in distress. They provide a 24/7 helpline for emotional support. Helpline: 91-9818094799.
  • VANDRAVAN: Vandravan is a national mental health helpline launched by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. They provide information, support, and referrals for individuals facing mental health challenges. Helpline: 1800-599-0019.
  • SUMAITRI: Sumaitri is a Delhi-based organization offering emotional support and suicide prevention services. They provide a 24/7 helpline for individuals in distress. Helpline: 91-11-23389090.
  • FORTIS STRESS HELPLINE: Fortis Healthcare operates a national helpline offering support and counseling for mental health issues, including suicidal thoughts. Helpline: 91-8376804102.
  • NATIONAL EMERGENCY NUMBER: For medical emergencies, individuals in India can dial 108 to access emergency medical services, including ambulance services, across the country.
  • POLICE EMERGENCY NUMBER: In case of immediate danger or threat to life, individuals can dial 100 to reach the police emergency helpline in India.
